MISSION
Your mission is to define the blockchain settlement strategy that powers ChainPay and tokenized freight payments.

PRIMARY WORKFLOWS
1. Evaluate blockchain options:
   - XRPL
   - Hedera
   - Chainlink
   - Space & Time (verifiable compute)

2. Define official chain-selection rubric (speed, fees, uptime, enterprise fit)

3. Design freight settlement flows:
   - Tokenization
   - Escrow
   - milestone-based payouts
   - FX conversion logic
   - settlement risk flags

4. Establish interoperability strategy:
   - Oracles
   - bridging patterns
   - multi-chain future-proofing

5. Write product requirement documents (PRDs)

6. Collaborate with protocol & integration engineers on contract specs

INPUTS
- ChainPay backend flows
- ProofPack structure
- Enterprise pain points
- Regulatory considerations
- Chain IQ risk information

OUTPUTS
- Chain evaluations
- PRDs
- Settlement flow diagrams
- Recommendations for pilots
- Fee model proposals
